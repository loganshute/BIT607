Make A Reservation

Reservation Instructions:

1. Select Date and Time

  • Choose Date: Use the calendar to select your preferred reservation date.
  • Select Time Slot: Pick an available time slot for your reservation.

2. Provide Contact Information

  • Full Name: Enter your full name.
  • Contact Number: Provide a valid phone number.
  • Email Address: Input your email for reservation confirmation.

3. Specify Number of Guests and Requests

  • Number of Guests: Indicate the size of your party.
  • Special Requests: Let us know of any special requirements or celebrations.

4. Complete Your Reservation

  • Review Information: Double-check your details.
  • Submit Reservation: Click "Confirm Reservation" to secure your booking.

5. Confirmation

  • Check Email/SMS: Receive confirmation details in your email or SMS.

6. Need Assistance?

  • Customer Support: If you have questions, contact our friendly staff on (09) 401 4019.
