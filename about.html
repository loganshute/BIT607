About Us

The Origin of Haukai

Amara and Basil's journey to establish Haukai began with their deep appreciation for the natural beauty of the Bay of Islands. The pristine waters, lush landscapes, and the vibrant culture of the Māori people served as the canvas for their culinary aspirations. The name "Haukai" itself reflects their connection to the elements, translating to "food of the wind" in Māori, symbolizing the spirit and vitality they sought to infuse into their restaurant.

Culinary Philosophy

At Haukai, the culinary philosophy is a harmonious blend of innovation and tradition. Amara and Basil believe in letting the natural flavors of locally sourced ingredients shine while incorporating modern techniques to create a dining experience that is both authentic and cutting-edge. Their commitment to sustainability extends beyond the kitchen, as they prioritize partnerships with local farmers and fishermen to ensure the freshest produce and seafood.

The Symbolism of the Menu

Every dish on the menu at Haukai tells a story—an ode to the sea, the land, and the Māori ancestors. From the carefully selected herbs to the choice of proteins, each ingredient is a tribute to the cultural tapestry that has woven its way into Amara and Basil's lives. The menu showcases not only the culinary prowess of the chefs but also their deep respect for the Māori spirits and traditions.

The Magical Setting

The physical space of Haukai is designed to be an extension of the Bay of Islands' enchanting atmosphere. The décor reflects the natural elements that surround the restaurant, with earthy tones, marine-inspired accents, and indigenous artwork. The ambiance is carefully curated to transport guests into a realm where they can feel the gentle whispers of the wind and the ancient stories carried by the waves.

Community Engagement

Beyond serving exceptional cuisine, Amara and Basil are actively involved in community initiatives. They organize events that celebrate Māori culture, collaborate with local artists, and support environmental causes to give back to the community that has embraced Haukai.

Owners' Story and Vision

Amara and Basil's tale is one of serendipity and a shared love for both adventure and the diverse flavors of the Bay of Islands. Their culinary journey was born out of their exploration of the rich marine life surrounding the bay, where every dive and discovery deepened their connection not only with each other but also with the spirit of the Māori people.

Drawn to the traditions of the indigenous Māori, Amara and Basil envisioned a restaurant that would serve as a tribute to the flavors that had become a part of their love story. With a shared passion for the culinary arts and a profound respect for Māori traditions, they set out to create Haukai—an embodiment of their journey, an expression of their connection with the sea, and a celebration of the Māori spirits that had guided them.

For Amara and Basil, Haukai is more than a restaurant; it's a manifestation of destiny and a testament to the extraordinary path that led them to this culinary venture. Each dish on the menu is crafted with love and reverence, infused with ingredients that carry the whispers of Māori traditions. Their vision is to share the magic of the Bay of Islands with both locals and tourists, inviting them to partake in a culinary experience that transcends the ordinary.

Haukai is not just a dining establishment; it's a journey through the flavors of the bay, a celebration of love, and a homage to the Māori heritage that inspires every aspect of the restaurant.

Staff Profiles

Aroha & Tane (Owners)

Aroha, with her deep connection to Māori heritage, serves as the culinary soul of Haukai. Her journey into the world of Māori kai started with her grandmother's recipes, and she now leads the kitchen with a commitment to preserving and evolving these traditions.

Tane, the creative force, brings a contemporary twist to the menu. With a background in international cuisine, he's passionate about creating dishes that appeal to diverse palates while respecting the essence of Māori flavors.

Sandi (Staff Member)

Sandi is the heart and soul of Haukai's warm hospitality. With a genuine passion for customer service, she ensures that every guest feels welcomed and embraced by the restaurant's unique atmosphere.

As the face behind the phone bookings, Sandi is delighted about the transition to an online system. Her commitment to providing a seamless experience for customers is unwavering, and she looks forward to freeing up valuable time to enhance the overall dining experience.
