About the Owners:

In the heart of the Bay of Islands in New Zealand, Amara Kerehoma and Basil Kerehoma, a dynamic couple with an insatiable appetite for adventure and flavor, embarked on a culinary journey that would enchant the taste buds of locals and tourists alike.

Their love story unfolded against the backdrop of the bay, where Amara and Basil spent countless hours exploring the rich marine life and cultivating a deep connection with the sea. Inspired by the traditions of the Māori, the indigenous people of New Zealand, they dreamt of creating a restaurant that would embody the essence of their shared passion.

Amara and Basil felt a calling to bring these flavors to life, paying homage to the Māori spirits that had guided their journey. With a sense of destiny, they opened the doors to "Haukai," their restaurant by the bay, where each dish was crafted with love. The menu, infused with ingredients that held the whispers of the Māori traditions, became a testament to their connection with the divine and the extraordinary journey that led them to share the magic of the Bay of Islands with the world.
