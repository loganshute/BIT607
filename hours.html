Important Notice:

We value our customers and want to keep you informed about our restaurant schedule.

Public Holiday Closure:

We will be closed on public holidays, including Christmas Day, to allow our staff to celebrate with their families.

Matariki Celebration:

We are excited to announce that we will be open for a special Matariki celebration! Matariki is a significant event in the Māori calendar, marking the start of the Māori New Year. Join us for a festive and cultural experience. Learn more about Matariki.

Private Events:

Looking to host a private event? Whether it's a birthday party, corporate gathering, or any special occasion, we offer private event services. You can book our venue for private events on both our open and closed days.

We appreciate your understanding and continued support. For more details and reservations, feel free to contact us.
